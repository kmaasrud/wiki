~ Ideal gas

An ideal gas is a gas consisting of particles that do not interact with eachother (particles can interact with elastic or point-like collisions while still following the ideal gas model.)1 The model is purely theoretical, but a useful approximation in many cases. All ideal gases follow the ideal gas law.
